Packet Tracer Challenge Lab

Built a simulated multi-LAN, multi-WAN enterprise network using Cisco Packet Tracer with 2800 series routers and 2950 switches. Configured IP addresses, RIP routing, passwords, and remote switch management to ensure full connectivity and security.

  • Configured 3 WAN and 5 LAN networks with correct IP addressing.
  • Implemented RIP routing protocol for automated route sharing.
  • Secured devices with encrypted passwords and console/VTY access.
  • Verified network functionality with ping and tracert commands.
